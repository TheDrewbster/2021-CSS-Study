Minecraft is filled to the brim with a wide variety of enemies and other entities, all referred to as "mobs". Mobs can either be hostile, passive, or neutral. Hostile mobs attack on sight. Passive mobs will never attack you, they are defenseless. Neutral mobs will not attack you, unless provoked by the player. Some mobs are based on real world animals, and some are mythical creatures.
