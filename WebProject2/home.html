Minecraft is a game loved by many, renowned for it's sandbox and captivating gameplay. The game is accessible on many platforms, from mobile phones, consoles, to PC. Initially released in 2011, Minecraft has come a very long way since. To this day, Minecraft is still going strong, being played by millions of new and returning players. On the topic of new players, we can all relate to being in that position, and the frustrations that follow. So, here is a fairly simple introductory guide to the game. Happy mining!
